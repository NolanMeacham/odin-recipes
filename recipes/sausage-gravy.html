My Favorite Sausage Gravy

Description

Nothing puts a smile on my face in the morning faster than a plate of delicious and savory sausage gravy over buttered toast. It's simple to make and will leave you satisfied and ready for the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) package of breakfast sausage
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Stir butter into the pan until melted
  3. Add flour and stir until smooth. Reduce heat to medium and cook until light brown
  4. Slowly add milk and stir with a whisk until thickened. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes. If gravy becomes too thick, stir in a little more milk.
  6. Prepare bread in the toaster to desired level
  7. Place toast on a plate and cover with sausage gravy.
  8. Prepare to have the best day of your life!

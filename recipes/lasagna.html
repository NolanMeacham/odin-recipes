Word's Best Lasagna

Description

Good lasagna can be challenging and time consuming to make but the smile on everyone's faces at the dinner table makes it well worth the time required.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Use a combination of Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • Parsley, Basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg (to help bind the ricotta)

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with tin foil and bake at 375 degrees F, 25 minutes covered, 25 minutes uncovered
  6. Let the Lasagna cool before serving and then, Enjoy!
